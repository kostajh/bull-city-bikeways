Bull City Bikeways

This is a map of bike routes used by folks in Durham, NC. All of the routes you see were entered by people like you - and we hope you'll add your route to the mix too!

Add your routes

Getting your routes on the map can be done in two simple steps. The whole process should take 5-15 minutes:

  1. Draw your route
  2. Submit your data

Repeat this process for as many routes as you care to submit. Having trouble with this process? Please e-mail us and we'll see what we can do.

If your route is already on the map, but you need to make a change go to the data page for the site, find your entry, and click "Edit" (you'll need to sign up for a free account at Github to do this). Or e-mail us with your requested changes.

Use cases

We think this map will be useful to you if:

  • you want to start cycling to work/school or for running errands but aren't sure about a good route to your destination
  • you are already riding but are looking for a safer, alternate route
  • you want to partner up with someone for your commute
  • you're a transport planner who wants to know where people are cycling.

Compliments and criticism

Your suggestions and critiques are most welcome. You can e-mail us, file an issue in the bug tracker, or make the change yourself - the site and its data are free and open source.
