Bull City Bikeways

What am I looking at?

You're looking at a map of bike routes used by folks in Durham, NC. All of the routes you see were entered by people like you - and we hope you'll add your route to the mix too!

OK, this looks neat, but what's the point?

We think this map will be useful to you if:

  • you want to start cycling to work/school or for running errands but aren't sure about a good route to your destination
  • you are already riding but are looking for a safer, alternate route
  • you want to partner up with someone for your commute
  • you're a transport planner who wants to know where people are cycling.

How do I get my route on the map?

Getting your route on the map can be done in two simple steps. The whole process should take 5-15 minutes:

1. Draw your route

  1. Go to GeoJSON.io
  2. Click "OSM" at the bottom left corner of the page.
  3. Click the "Polyline" tool on the left hand side of the page. Zoom in to your starting point, then click on the map to start drawing your route. Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move the map around. Note: the line will not automatically follow a roadway (we don't have Google's resources, sorry). So you will have to click a lot to create a line that follows a curvy road. Please try to keep your line on the road, otherwise we can't post your route on the map.
  4. Done drawing your route? Great! CLick the "Save" icon at the top of the page. You should now see text at the top left of the page that looks like anonymous/5809d4d2a044032744d7. Don't lose this - you'll need that information in the next step.

2. Fill out a form

  1. Go to the data submission form.
  2. Fill out the form. You'll need the code from the previous step. Once you're done, press submit. We'll get your data posted onto the map within a few days! Thanks!

Having trouble with this process? Please e-mail us and we'll see what we can do.
